Everything you need to know about how acceptorIQ works.

The review

We analyse your merchant statement — the document your payment provider sends you each month — to understand exactly what you're paying, how it compares to the market, and where there may be opportunities to reduce costs. The review is produced by AI and then checked by a payments specialist before it reaches you.

Your data

Yes. Your statement is transmitted securely and stored in an encrypted database. It is used only to generate your report — it is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for any other purpose. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@acceptoriq.com.au.
